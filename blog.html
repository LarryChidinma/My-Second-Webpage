Understanding Holistic Wellness

Holistic wellness is a multifaceted approach that acknowledges the interdependence of our physical, mental, emotional, and spiritual well-being.
Unlike traditional medicine, which often focuses solely on treating symptoms, holistic wellness addresses the root causes of imbalance,
seeking to restore harmony and vitality to the entire person.
